Our Love Story

First Meet

22 Oct 2021

Sometimes, love finds you when you least expect it. For Meghan and Austin, it all started at Texas Roadhouse. Austin was working there at the time, and Meghan had come in for dinner with a friend after attending a recital. As fate would have it, Austin was their server that evening. What began as casual conversation soon turned into something more—an unmistakable spark that neither of them could ignore. Every time Austin stopped by the table, their connection deepened through easy laughter and warm exchanges. On a whim, Meghan decided to leave her phone number on the receipt, trusting a feeling she couldn't quite explain. The very next morning, Austin reached out—and from that moment on, their story began to unfold. What started as a chance meeting turned into a love that neither of them could have imagined, leading them here, to this beautiful new chapter.

First Date

27 Oct 2021

For their very first date, Austin took Meghan to dinner in charming Germantown, Wisconsin, at a cozy spot called The Precinct Tap and Table. Over shared stories and heartfelt conversation, the evening unfolded with the ease of two people meant to find each other. Not long after, their second date took a more playful turn—an outing to the dog park with Meghan’s beloved pup, Charlie. It was there, watching Austin and Charlie bond with such natural joy, that Meghan’s heart quietly whispered what she already knew: he was the one. And as for Charlie—he couldn’t have agreed more.

Proposal

10 Jun 2023

The proposal was nothing short of perfect—surrounded by love, laughter, and many of the people who mean the most to them. At the beginning of summer, Austin planned a getaway to his Uncle Kevin’s cabin on the shores of Lake Superior. Under the sweet disguise of a simple vacation, he gathered many of Meghan’s closest friends and her siblings for a weekend filled with memories—and a surprise that would change everything. One afternoon, during a peaceful hike down to the lake, Meghan was lost in thought, admiring the beauty around her. She even mused to herself that this would be the perfect spot for a proposal someday—completely unaware that her "someday" was only moments away. As the group reached the water’s edge, someone suggested taking a photo to remember the day. They decided Meghan and Austin should go first, so the two stood by the lake while everyone else took out their phones and gathered on the stairs. After a few pictures, Austin quietly stepped back. When Meghan turned around, she found him down on one knee, holding a stunning moss agate ring, his eyes full of love and intention. Surprised, overwhelmed, and overjoyed, Meghan said yes—surrounded by cheers, happy tears, and the warm embrace of their closest friends and family. It was a moment as breathtaking and unforgettable as the life they’re now building together.

Engagement

10 Jun 2023

After getting engaged, Meghan and Austin—along with their beloved dog Charlie—made the move to Illinois. Meghan began her Master’s program while Austin continued working toward his Bachelor’s degree. Over the past two years, they’ve built a life full of adventure; traveling to Aruba and the Dominican Republic, and making unforgettable memories camping under the stars. Now, as they prepare for their next chapter, they’re excited to return home to Wisconsin!
